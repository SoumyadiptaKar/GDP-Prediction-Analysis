Social Indicators Overview

This dataset contains 12 comprehensive social and economic indicators spanning 1960-2023 across 154 countries:

Economic Indicators
  • GDP: Gross Domestic Product per capita (USD)
  • Population: Total population count
  • Migration: Net migration (positive = immigration)
Health & Demographics
  • Life Exp.: Life expectancy at birth (years)
  • Infant Mort.: Infant mortality rate per 1,000 births
  • Female %: Female population percentage
  • Male %: Male population percentage
Development Indicators
  • Internet %: Internet penetration rate
  • Education %: School enrollment rate
  • HCI: Human Capital Index (0-1 scale)
  • Urban %: Urban population percentage
Tip: Use the focus metric filter to highlight specific indicators, or sort columns by clicking headers. The table displays the most recent 5 years of data by default.
